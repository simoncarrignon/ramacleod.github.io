  • 13:55–14:20 Charlotte Houldcroft: Evaluating the adaptive immune response
    Abstract

    When we identify ancient pathogens in human or animal remains, sequence analysis often suggests changes in key pathogen surface proteins with roles in cell entry and/or immune recognition. Using methods from transplant and pandemic virology, I will discuss methods to assess the adaptive immune response to ancient or extinct viruses without the need for live - potentially deadly - isolates. I will briefly discuss how this can be integrated with knowledge on human immunogenetic change over time.
